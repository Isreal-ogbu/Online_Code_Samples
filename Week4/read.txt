Installation of JDK/SDK and IDE (IntelliJ)
Step 1: Download the JDK:
Open a web browser and go to the Oracle JDK download page: Java Downloads | Oracle
Click on the "Java SE Development Kit 20 Downloads" section.
Accept the license agreement by selecting the checkbox next to "Accept License Agreement".
Choose the JDK installer according to your operating system. For Windows, download the appropriate JDK installer for your system (x64 for 64-bit or x86 for 32-bit).
Click on the download link to start downloading the JDK installer.
